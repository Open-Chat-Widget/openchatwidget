Open Chat Widget lets you bring a ChatGPT-like AI chat experience inside of your website. Build your own custom AI agent and modify the chat UI. Completely customize your chat experience. Open Chat Widget is entirely free, open source, and self-hosted. You own the entire stack. 

If you're looking to provide agentic chat experiences on your website, this is the project. 


### Example use cases
- AI customer service agent - Open Chat Widget is a free alternative to Intercom's Fin AI and Tidio chat. 
- Knowledge base & documentation search - help your users answer any questions about your documentation, or anything about your knowledge base. 
- In product onboarding - chat assistant to help users onboard to your dashboard / product. 
- Bookings and task automation - AI agent to schedule meetings, track orders, book appointments, and more.
